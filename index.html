Mind Diet

for two

The MIND diet combines two of the healthiest diets in the world—the Mediterranean diet and the DASH diet—and can improve brain health and lower your odds of developing age-related problems. This comprehensive cookbook features MIND diet recipes made for just two people. You'll both enjoy eating foods that protect and nourish the brain without all the leftovers.

The power of the MIND diet

The MIND Diet explains the science behind mental fitness in an approachable and understandable way. More importantly, this helpful guide presents an easy-to-follow program for keeping your mind sharp by eating the right foods and avoiding brain-harming ones.

The Mediterranean-DASH Intervention for Neurodegenerative Delay, or the MIND diet, is a brain-building way to eat that's proven to treat hypertension, support your brain health, and reduce the risk of Alzheimer's disease and dementia—all while enjoying delicious and nutritious meals.

Kick-start your MIND diet with an easy step-by-step guide, plus a 7-day meal plan—complete with shopping lists. And dig in to 85 tasty beginner recipes that keep your brain (and body) healthy and happy.
